The Preferences Dialog: Identity Panel

Accessing the Preferences Dialog Identity Panel

You can access the preferences dialog from the Edit: Preferences menu item, then by clicking on the Identity tab.

The Identity panel sets your identity for outgoing messages.

Real Name

Preference File Key: newsagent.identity.realname

Your full name.

Email Address

Preference File Key: newsagent.identity.email

Your email address. This field must contain something before you will be allowed to post messages.

Organisation

Preference File Key: newsagent.identity.organisation

Your organisation. You can leave this field blank if you wish.

OK / Cancel

Save or abort changes to preferences.
