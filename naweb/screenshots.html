The Main Window

[New]: The New Preference Dialog Style

Preference dialogs now look like hybrid Netscape/MS Office preference dialogs, with a tree control on the left hand side and panels on the right hand side headed by etched lines. The layout should be a lot neater than the old dialogs, hopefully.

The screenshot (gif, 13.3Kb) is of the new Message Viewer preferences in 1.1.0 build 42 onwards. These preferences are currently stored, but the viewer doesn't use them yet.
