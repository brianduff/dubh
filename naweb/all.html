Implementation

Not much to say really. I've been working on Internationalising the strings in the application, and at the moment I even have some test German and French resource files. Everything seems to work quite nicely.

I spent most of today (6 Mar 98) working on the Signature options (the two small dialogs in the left bottom corner). Most of the work was getting the list to work consistently, and the behind the scenes code which stores the information in user preference files. These, coincidentally are the two Internationalised dialogs so far.
